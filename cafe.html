Passport Processing

New, renewal, or replacement — we've got you.

We assist with all Uganda passport categories through the official government portal. Bring the right documents and we do the rest — fast, accurate, no stress.

New Application

  • Original National ID (both sides)
  • Original Birth Certificate
  • 2 recent passport photos (white background)
  • Application fee receipt

Renewal

  • Old passport (original)
  • National ID (original)
  • 2 recent passport photos
  • Renewal fee receipt

Lost / Replacement

  • Police report (original)
  • National ID (original)
  • Affidavit from court
  • 2 passport photos
  • Replacement fee receipt

Driving Permits & Visas

Driving Permit — What to Bring

  • National ID (original)
  • Existing permit (if renewal)
  • Medical certificate (class A/B)
  • Application fee receipt
  • 2 passport photos

Visa Assistance

  • Valid passport (6+ months)
  • Invitation letter or hotel booking
  • Return ticket confirmation
  • Bank statement (last 3 months)
  • Passport photos as required
